How To Train Your Pet Dog?
  1. Dogs learn a lot like little kids. They are close in intelligence to human two-year-olds.
  2. Adaptive learning is how well your dog learns from their surroundings and the environment around them to solve problems.
  3. Both aversive- and reward-based training have been proven to work.
How To Manage Your Pets?
  1. Provide a protected and clean living environment for your dog.
  2. Awalys keep fresh water available.
  3. Feed a quality diet and prevent obesity.
What Is The Best Grooming For Your Pets?
  1. Regularly brush your dog's coat to prevent matting.
  2. Trim your dog's hair but use caution.
  3. Safely trim your dog's nails.
What Are Those Requirements For Sitting Pets?
  1. Proven experience working as a pet sitter.
  2. Working knowledge of animal behavior.
  3. The ability to perform animal CPR and first aid.
